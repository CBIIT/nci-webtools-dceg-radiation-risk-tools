Guidance on 90% Uncertainty Range

A 90% uncertainty range implies that there is high confidence that the true value is contained between the lower and upper values of the range.

Within this calculator, the 90% uncertainty range is obtained from 1,000 simulations of dose and risk, using probability distributions to represent the state of knowledge in all uncertain model inputs.
